How to set the counter?
Open index.html using any text editor, at line# 170 find the "date:" and change it to your launching date and time.

How to change the brand name?
Open index.html using any text editor, search for "Coming Soon" and replace it with your brand or company name all over the document.

 

How to change the branding colors?
Top navigation menu, links and Google map colors are fully customizable and below how to change each one of them..
Top navigation menu, find "pixelstrawberry.css" in "css" folder, open it and goto line# 115, change the background-color to your brand color. Do the same at line# 60.
Links, find "pixelstrawberry.css" in "css" folder, open it and goto line# 44 then change the color code to youre desired one, to change the hover color, goto line# 53 and change it you another color.
Google map colors, find "pixelstrawberry.js" in "js" folder, open it and goto line# 74, change the color to your desired one.

 

How to change the map location?
Find "pixelstrawberry.js" in "js" folder, open it and goto line# 25, change google.maps.LatLng(38.929903,-77.052762) to your accurate business location which you can find by visiting maps.google.com, search for your location, goto settings "little gear icon at the bottom", share and embed map, at the end of the link you find these numbers.

 

How to change the pin icon in the map?
Find an image named "pin.png" in the "img" folder, replace it with your favourite pin image.

 

How to change the home page background image?
Find an image named "intro-bg.jpg" in the "img" folder, replace it with your favourite image.

 

How to change the middle section background image?
Find an image named "downloads-bg.jpg" in the "img" folder, replace it with your favourite image.

 

How to change the dark background pattern?
Find an image named "stardust.png" & "stardust_@2X.png" in the "img" folder, replace them with your images.

 

How to change the dark background pattern?
Find an icon named "favicon.ico" in the "img" folder, replace it with your logo icon. If you don't have one, you can create your own by visiting http://www.favicon.cc website.
